I'm Bo.

I work as an Engineering Manager at @JinaAI_. My team aims to build the best neural retrievers in the world.

Our journey started with fine-tuning embedding models using finetuner.jina.ai.

We expanded our task to training models from scratch, and luckily, we witnessed the rise of RAG.

As of today, we have released general text embedding models: jina-embeddings-v1, v2, v3; late interaction retrievers: jina-colbert-v1, v2; and multimodal embedding models: jina-clip-v1. These models have been downloaded over 10 million times on Huggingface.

Although speed is key in today's AI landscape, we periodically submit our work to academic conferences and workshops, such as EMNLP and ICML.

Before joining @JinaAI_, I worked as a Machine Learning Engineer and Research Intern, and I earned my MSc from TU Delft, Delft, the Netherlands, and my BSc from Lanzhou University, Lanzhou, China.
